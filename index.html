Why I want to visit Chongqing (eventually)

Introduction

Chongqing is the largest city in China, with a landmass of around 82,000km2, making it around 1/3 of the size of the entire UK and slightly larger than Austria, and is home to around 30,000,000 people. The city is renowned for its engineering, technology, transportation and history (reference).

Below are some images that illustrate the different sights common in Chongqing but perhaps surreal to the rest of the world.

Reasons I want to go

  1. As seen in the leftmost image, the drone light shows every Saturday are spectacular.
  2. The city itself is constructed in vertical layers, and is structurally impressive.
  3. Shopping plazas such as The Ring (rightmost image) are vast spaces and are rather uniquely designed, the particular example of The Ring has a jungle theme inside.
  4. Aside from these, the entire city has a unique sci-fi aesthetic that I find appealing.
